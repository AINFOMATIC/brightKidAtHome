Playgroup

Our playgroup program focuses on nurturing the development of the absorbent mind, physical co- ordination and gross motor skills by offering hands on learning using smart books, Montessori materials, Art & Craft materials. Children enjoy and have fun while interacting with teachers and peers. Parents get the academic support from our team and child friendly Apps and portal.

Key area's of development are:

  • Spoken language
  • Social skills
  • Gross & Fine Motor skills
  • Self Help skills
  • Creativity skills
  • Cognitive skills
  • Perceptual skills

Enrollment

We have very simple, quick, safe and full proof method which can be completed in just 5 mins. Select from the below the program type and duration of program that you are looking for. These choice are combinations of type of programs (Basic preschool or with Montessori upgrade) and the durations ( Full Term, Mid Term and Short Term). So these are:

  • Basic Preschool for the full term.
  • Montessori Upgrade Program for full-term and additionally includes both Montessori Box 1 and Montessori Box 2.
  • Basic Preschool for Mid- Term (starts in the middle of term general for 3-6 months after commencement of the term)
  • Montessori upgrade program for Mid- Term (starts in the middle of term general for 3-6 months after commencement of the term) and additionally includes only Montessori Box 2.
  • Basic Preschool for Short Term (only for 3 months starting from the beginning of the session).
  • Montessori upgrade program for Short Term (only for 3 months starting from the beginning of the session) and additionally includes only Montessori Box 1.
